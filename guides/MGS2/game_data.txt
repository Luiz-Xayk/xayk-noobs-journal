# Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty - Game Data
# Format: AREA | VISUAL_DESCRIPTION | OBJECTIVE | ITEMS | NEXT_STEP | TIPS

==============================================================================
TANKER CHAPTER - SOLID SNAKE
==============================================================================

## AFT DECK (Starting Area)
- Visual: External ship deck in rain, green cargo crates, large pipes, spotlight beams
- Objective: Infiltrate the tanker undetected
- Items: None in this starting area
- Next: Go down the hatch to the ship interior (Navigation Deck)
- Tip: Rain muffles footsteps. Use crates as cover.

## AFT DECK - SPOTLIGHT AREA
- Visual: Area with rotating spotlights, patrolling guards, stacked crates
- Objective: Pass through spotlights without being seen
- Items: Ration behind crates on the right
- Next: Continue to the hatch at the end of the deck
- Tip: Wait for spotlights to pass before moving. If spotted, hide until Alert ends.

## DECK-A, CREW'S QUARTERS
- Visual: Narrow internal corridor, cabin doors on both sides, artificial lighting
- Objective: Pass through without alerting guards in the rooms
- Items: Ration in corridor locker, M9 Tranquilizer ammo
- Next: Proceed to Deck-A, Crew's Lounge
- Tip: Guards may exit cabins. Knock on wall to lure and knock out.

## DECK-A, CREW'S LOUNGE
- Visual: Room with sofas, TV on, bar, vending machines
- Objective: Pass through the room undetected
- Items: Ration in vending machine (shoot it)
- Next: Stairs to Deck-B
- Tip: Guard watches TV - pass behind him silently.

## DECK-B, CREW'S QUARTERS
- Visual: Another quarters corridor, more patrolling guards
- Objective: Continue descending through the ship
- Items: Pentazemin in locker
- Next: Deck-C
- Tip: Use M9 to knock out guards from distance.

## DECK-C, CREW'S QUARTERS
- Visual: Similar corridor, but with more guards
- Objective: Reach Deck-D
- Items: Bandage
- Next: Deck-D
- Tip: Crawl under windows to avoid being seen.

## DECK-D, CREW'S QUARTERS
- Visual: Last quarters deck before Engine Room
- Objective: Find the Engine Room entrance
- Items: Chaff Grenade
- Next: Engine Room
- Tip: Guard sleeps in one of the cabins - stay quiet.

## ENGINE ROOM
- Visual: Huge room with giant machines, metal catwalks, very noisy
- Objective: Cross the engine room
- Items: Ration, M9 ammo
- Next: Deck-2, Port
- Tip: Machine noise covers your footsteps, but guards patrol the catwalks.

## DECK-2, PORT (Cargo Hold)
- Visual: Cargo area with containers, screen showing Metal Gear RAY
- Objective: Photograph Metal Gear RAY
- Items: Camera already equipped
- Next: Take 4 photos of RAY as Otacon instructs
- Tip: Use camera with R1. Photograph: front, "MARINES", "RAY", and one of containers.

## BOSS: OLGA GURLUKOVICH
- Visual: External deck, storm, tarp covering area, armed Olga
- Objective: Defeat Olga in combat
- Items: USP (pick up after defeating Olga)
- Next: Return to Engine Room after cutscene
- Tip: Shoot the light to blind her. When she hides, wait for her to reload before shooting.

## HOLDS - MARINE AREA
- Visual: Huge hold filled with Marines guarding Metal Gear RAY
- Objective: Take photos of RAY without being detected
- Items: None - photo area
- Next: After photos, cutscene with Ocelot
- Tip: Use upper catwalks. Photograph from above to avoid detection.

==============================================================================
PLANT CHAPTER - RAIDEN
==============================================================================

## STRUT A, DEEP SEA DOCK
- Visual: Underwater dock, entry pool, elevators
- Objective: Infiltrate Big Shell
- Items: M9 provided by Colonel
- Next: Strut A Roof
- Tip: Your weapon is tranquilizer - does not kill, only puts to sleep.

## STRUT A, ROOF
- Visual: External rooftop, rain, catwalks connecting to other Struts
- Objective: Find the first hostage
- Items: Ration
- Next: Strut A, Pump Room or AB Connecting Bridge
- Tip: Guards patrol the roof - use cover.

## AB CONNECTING BRIDGE
- Visual: Bridge connecting Strut A and B, open to the sea
- Objective: Cross to Strut B
- Items: None
- Next: Strut B
- Tip: Careful with wind - can affect aim.

## STRUT B, TRANSFORMER ROOM
- Visual: Room with electric transformers, control panels
- Objective: Cross to BC Bridge
- Items: Chaff Grenade
- Next: BC Connecting Bridge
- Tip: Security cameras - use Chaff to disable.

## BC CONNECTING BRIDGE
- Visual: Long bridge over the sea
- Objective: Cross to Strut C
- Items: None
- Next: Strut C, Dining Hall - FORTUNE ENCOUNTER
- Tip: Cutscene with Fortune - she is invincible for now!

## STRUT C, DINING HALL
- Visual: Cafeteria with tables, kitchen nearby
- Objective: Find Peter Stillman
- Items: Coolant Spray (Peter gives to you)
- Next: Start disabling C4 bombs
- Tip: Peter teaches about the bombs - pay attention to instructions.

## MISSION: DISABLE C4 BOMBS
- Visual: Various locations in Struts A, B, C, D, E, F
- Objective: Find and disable all C4 bombs with Coolant
- Items: Bomb sensor (Peter provides)
- Next: After disabling all, return to Strut A
- Tip: Use sensor to find bombs. Spray with Coolant until frozen.

## STRUT F, WAREHOUSE
- Visual: Large warehouse with crates, tall shelves
- Objective: Find the Nikita Launcher
- Items: Nikita Launcher, ammo
- Next: Use Nikita to reach inaccessible areas
- Tip: Nikita is a controllable missile - useful for hitting distant targets.

## BOSS: FATMAN
- Visual: Heliport (Strut E), Fatman on roller skates, bombs scattered
- Objective: Defeat Fatman
- Items: Nothing during fight
- Next: After defeating, disable the last bomb on his body
- Tip: Shoot legs to knock him down. When he falls, shoot the head. Disable bombs he plants!

## SHELL 1 CORE
- Visual: Interior of Shell 1 structure, metal corridors
- Objective: Infiltrate core and find the President
- Items: Various along the way
- Next: Find AK Suppressor, continue ascending
- Tip: Many guards - intense stealth gameplay.

## BOSS: VAMP (FIRST ENCOUNTER)
- Visual: Pool in Shell 1 Core B2
- Objective: Defeat Vamp
- Items: Nothing during fight
- Next: Continue to find Emma
- Tip: Shoot head when he emerges from water. Avoid the knives!

## SHELL 2 CORE
- Visual: Structure similar to Shell 1
- Objective: Find Emma Emmerich
- Items: Various
- Next: Escort Emma back
- Tip: Emma fears water and insects - guide her carefully.

## BOSS: VAMP (SECOND ENCOUNTER)
- Visual: Oil Fence
- Objective: Protect Emma while she crosses
- Items: PSG1 Sniper Rifle
- Next: After defeating Vamp, cutscene
- Tip: Use Sniper to shoot Vamp. Protect Emma!

## ARSENAL GEAR
- Visual: Interior of Arsenal Gear, futuristic metal corridors
- Objective: Escape with Snake
- Items: High Frequency Blade (HF Blade)
- Next: Fight through Metal Gear RAYs
- Tip: Now you have the sword! Use it to cut enemies.

## BOSS: METAL GEAR RAYs
- Visual: Arena with multiple Metal Gear RAY
- Objective: Destroy the RAYs
- Items: Stinger Missile
- Next: After destroying enough, cutscene
- Tip: Shoot knees, then head when it opens. Snake helps!

## BOSS: SOLIDUS SNAKE
- Visual: Top of Federal Hall, New York
- Objective: Defeat Solidus in sword combat
- Items: HF Blade
- Next: End of game
- Tip: Block with L1, attack when he drops guard. Attack combos work well.
